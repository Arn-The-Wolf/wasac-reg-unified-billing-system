WASAC / REG Utility Billing — ERD

Aligned with JPA entities in rw.wasac.reg.billing.entity (Spring Boot 3.5)

Critique of the Previous Diagram

  • Wrong / missing tables — omitted otps, tariff_tiers, fixed_charges, taxes, penalties, and the users ↔ customers link.
  • Incorrect column names — e.g. full_names (actual: full_name), bill_reference (actual: reference), month/year (actual: billing_month/billing_year).
  • Phantom columns — customers.code, customers.country_code, meter_readings.consumption, bills.due_date, bills.tariff_id, payments.payment_reference, payments.customer_id, notifications.is_read do not exist in the codebase.
  • Flattened tariff model — real design uses versioned tariffs (FLAT or TIERED + tariff_tiers) and separate fixed_charges, taxes, penalties tables resolved at bill-generation time — not stored as FK on bills.
  • Wrong notification entity — table is customer_notifications with sent_at and month_year; no read/unread flag.
  • Missing payment workflow — payments.status (PENDING → APPROVED/REJECTED) and notes are core to finance approval.
  • Wrong types — monetary fields are decimal(12,2) (BigDecimal), not double.
  • Missing uniqueness — one reading per meter per billing period; one bill per meter per billing period.

Corrected ERD (13 tables, actual FK relationships)

Logical only (no FK): tariffs, fixed_charges, taxes, and penalties are looked up by BillServiceImpl using meter type and billing period — amounts are snapshotted on the bill row.
